About

Purpose

This application was created to make brush and weed management recommendations from Texas A&M AgriLife Extension publications easier to search and use in the field. Rather than manually searching through multiple reference tables, users can quickly locate recommendations by plant species, herbicide, treatment method, or other criteria.

This application is intended as a convenience and educational reference. It is not an official publication of Texas A&M AgriLife Extension.

Data Sources

The recommendations presented in this application are based on educational materials published by Texas A&M AgriLife Extension, including:

  • Brush Busters
  • Chemical Weed/Brush Control Reference Guide

Where possible, recommendations are reproduced directly from these publications.

Important Information

Always read and follow the herbicide product label. If information in this application differs from the product label, follow the label.

Herbicide labels are legal documents. Always comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and label directions.
